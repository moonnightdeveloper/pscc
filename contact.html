Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9AM-6PM EST). For urgent matters, please use our live chat or phone support for immediate assistance.

Do you offer technical support for course materials?

Yes! We provide comprehensive technical support for all our courses and tutorials. Our support team includes experts in frontend development, backend systems, and database management who can help you with any technical challenges.

Can I get a refund for a course?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our billing department within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you offer corporate or team training?

Yes, we provide customized corporate training programs for teams. Contact our partnership team at partnerships@psc.com to discuss your organization's specific training needs and get a customized quote.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

You can report bugs through our GitHub issues page, via email at bugs@psc.com, or through the feedback form on our website. Please include as much detail as possible about the issue, including screenshots and steps to reproduce.
